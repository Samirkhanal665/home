Self Introduction

Hello, my name is Samir Khanal, and I am from Palpa, Nepal. I completed my diploma in Civil Engineering in Nepal and am currently studying Information Systems and Security at Tongmyong University in Busan, South Korea. Being a foreign student here in Korea has been an exciting and enriching experience, both academically and culturally.

In addition to my academic work, I am passionate about sports, especially football and cricket, and I enjoy spending my weekends relaxing or learning new skills. My time in Korea has allowed me to grow not only academically but also personally, and I am excited about what the future holds.
